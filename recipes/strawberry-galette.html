Strawberry Galette

Description

An amazing summer treat and a crowd pleaser, strawberry gelettes are easy to make and worth the bit of hassle of preparing a homemade pastry and whipped cream. I use strawberries from my garden, sometimes frozen and vaccumed sealed as the season for good strawberries is quite short.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pastry recipe (save the other half for another preparation
  • 1 - 1 1/2 cup of good strawberries, whole or cut
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • pinch salt
  • whipped cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Roll out pastry on floured parchment into a round about 1/4 inch thick
  3. Mix strawberries with sugar and salt in a mixing bowl
  4. Top rolled out pastry with strawberry mixture and spread, leaving a border of pastry of about an inch or more
  5. Cut 5-8 incisions into the exposed pastry all around the mixture, evenly spaced.
  6. Fold exposed pastry towards the center. Pastry will overlap some.
  7. Bake on a baking sheet for 20-30 minutes, until the strawberry mixture is bubbly.
  8. When finished, let rest and cool for 10-20 minutes.
  9. Serve with whipped cream.
